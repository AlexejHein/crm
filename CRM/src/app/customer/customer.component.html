Users On the 'Users' page of the CRM app, users can be created and managed. During the creation or editing process, a password is assigned to each user. With this assigned password and their email address, users can subsequently log in to the app. In the detailed view of a user's profile, there is an overview of their pending tasks. This includes whether they have any outstanding 'To-dos' or appointments, or if there are any they need to schedule. This feature helps in monitoring and managing user activities and responsibilities within the app.
